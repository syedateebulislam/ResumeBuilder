  • About:

    A "resume" is a one to two page formal document that lists a job applicant's work experience, education and skills. A resume is designed to provide a detailed summary of an applicant's qualifications for a particular job - it is not usually meant to provide a complete picture. A good resume gives the potential employer enough information to believe the applicant is worth interviewing. A one-page cover letter, submitted along with the resume, can provide additional information about the applicant's qualifications.

  • Why is RESUME important?

    This is going to be your one and only chance to capture a potential employer's attention, or for your resume to be tossed into the file of those they don't want to pursue. The statistics are that the average employer only spends a few seconds looking at a resume, so it is imperative that it makes an impression.
